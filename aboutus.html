About Us

Coding Food Delivery

Three ladies decided it was time to share our passion for Asian and Western cuisine and coding activity with the rest of Indonesia and so we opened Coding Food Delivery, a restaurant and food service, situated in Jakarta, Indonesia. Providing an informal and welcoming atmosphere CFD aims to charm your taste buds with a carefully selected range of dishes. Borrowing a little from Asian and Western cultures, you’re encouraged to select several dishes at once so that you can sample a variety of both Asian and Western delights on offer. We believe that good food is key to achieving this goal and, in the same way that we make sure our customers eat healthily wherever they are. CFD specialises in Asian and Western food, offering and delivering dishes with fresh and selected food . A selection of alcoholic beverages is also on offer, including wine. Whether you’re looking for food and beverages to eat and drink with friends or hoping to impress someone special with great food, CFD will keep you happy. All the ingredients are selected with the utmost care so you can be sure that you’re eating the best that Indonesia has to offer.

Service

We aim to introduce you to the art of combination of Asian and Western Cuisine. Our experienced chefs use traditional techniques combined with fine quality ingredients to create an authentic experience. We take care to source from local reputable suppliers, many of whom we have known for many years. Each one source individual specialities which ensure we offer healthy dishes.
